The Rules of Dixit :

Phase 1 : The StoryTeller chooses one of his cards and gives you a hint to recognize it.
Phase 2 : All other players choose a card of their own that could also match the hint.
Phase 3 : All cards are now shuffled and revealed : the players have to find the card of the StoryTeller.
The goal for the StoryTeller is that some people will find his card, but not all of them. He his completely free on the hint and the informations he gives, but he should obviously try to make it neither too obvious nor too obscure.

The points :

  • 3pts for the StoryTeller if some but not all players find his card (0pts otherwise)
  • 2pts if you find the card of the StoryTeller
  • 1pt per player who voted for your card instead of the one of the StoryTeller

Send us your own custom cards :

Please make sure your card is vertical with a 2/3 ratio. 500x750 pixels is a good size.
Also make sure you have all the rights on the image ^^
Then send at jules.fouchy@ntymail.com either :
  • Your image in png or jpeg format
  • A p5 script. It will be run each time the card is drawn and if it involves some randomness the card will be different each round !
  • A fragment shader written in glsl (feature coming soon)
  • Got another way of doing art ? Get in touch with me and I might implement it !
